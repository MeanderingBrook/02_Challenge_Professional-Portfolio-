Escape the Manor of Doom!: A Python-Based Text Adventure

Description:  

Escape from the Manor of Doom is a downloadable Python-based, console text adventure for one Player. Through the Player's local terminal, the Player will explore an ancient manor filled with Monsters, Treaure, and... Death! Can you successfully penetrate the Manor of Doom and live?! (Note: Python 3 is required to run the game, after download.)
