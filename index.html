About

Good fortune has taken me around the world and across the country in a varied career to...

         Africa   •   Australia   •   Italy   •   Russia   •   D.C., San Francisco, Boston

My work began in Finance, and quickly incorporated Technology and Program Management while abroad.

A return to the States brought a renewed focus on Finance and introduced Application Product Management to my portfolio.

From an early stage in my career I've known enough coding to be dangerous -- mostly to myself, but occassionaly to innocent apps.

Of late, my priority has been Strategic Technology and diving deeply into Full-Stack Development.
